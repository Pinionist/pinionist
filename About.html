Pavle Milicevic is a seasoned VFX artist and compositor with extensive experience in commercial and advertising projects. Specializing in high-end visual effects and compositing work, Pavle has contributed to campaigns for major brands including Zubr, Biedronka, Toyota, Pepsi, LV Murakami, Powerade, Congstar, Piraeus Bank, Matchmasters, GMC and XTB.

His expertise spans from on-set supervision to final compositing, delivering polished visual storytelling across diverse projects ranging from character animation to product visualization. With a keen eye for detail and technical proficiency in industry-standard tools, Pavle brings creative visions to life through seamless integration of live-action footage and digital elements.
